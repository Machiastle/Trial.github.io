ABOUT ADVENA

ADVENA is a local basketball Team based in Plaridel, Bulacan. The Basket group was collectively founded by its members. The basketball group consists of a variety of players originating from various areas of Plaridel, Bulacan. With its player's shared loved for the game of basketball, they were able to create a bond deeper than the baskets they shoot, and higher than the rings they reach.
To this day, the group is still well and active, and aims to claim there very first championship title in this upcoming Tabang Basketball League. Stay tuned for their games and witness greatness and history in the making!
